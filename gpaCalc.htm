About this Page

This page, as you see is a GPA Calculator. It shows my knowledge mainly in JavaScript. It shows how to properly use a JavaScript form. This page demonstrates how to get info from certain objects in the form (like the text box) and also insert information back into the form. What happens is that this page collects your grades and transforms the letter grade into an integer (i.e. A = 4.0). Then it adds up all of those integers. After this, the code gets the number of classes you entered into the text box and divides that by the sum of the grades, giving your calculated GPA and inserting it into the disabled text box at the bottom.
